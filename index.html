INTRODUCTION

An overflow of water onto normally dry land.The in inundation of a normally dry area caused by rising water in an existing waterway. Such as a river,stream, or drainage ditch. Ponding of water at or near the point where the rain fall.Flooding is a longer term event than flash flooding.It may last days or weeks.

Flooding may ocur as an overflow of water from water bodies such as a river,lake,or ocean in which the water overtops or breaks resulting in some of that water escaping it's usual boundaries, or it may occur due to an accumulation of rainwater on saturated ground in an areal flood.

CAUSES OF FLOOD

It occur when drainage channels are fulled and the river and streams can no longer accommodates the excessive water generated by weather conditions the drainage channels then overtop their natural or artificial banks and water enters the surrounding lands to cause flooding

PRECAUTIONS

  • Continue to monitor the media for emergency information
  • Follow instruction from public safety office
  • Call 9-1-1 to report emergencies including downed power lines and gas leaks

ACTION PLAN DURING THE DISASTER

  • Stay away from electric poles and fallen power lines to avoid electrocution
  • Eat freshly cooked or dry food, Keep your food covered
  • Drink boiled/Chlorinated water

ACTION PLAN AFTER THE DISASTER

  • Don't use the toilet or tap water if the water lines/sevage pipes are damage
  • Do not drink tap water until advised by the health department that the water is safe to drink

When the water recedes it leaves sedument and nutrents behind on the floodplain this rich natural fertilize improves soil quality and has a positive effect on plant growth,their increasing productivily in the ecosystem
